Technical Specifications

Column and rafters of rigid frames are tapered built-up “I” section. All rigid frame connections are bolted. Columns and rafters are provided with welded end plates for anchoring to foundations and for member- to – member attachment.

Welded clips are provided for attachment of Purlins and girders, bracing, and other components.

Load bearing “post-and-beam” (P&B) end frames may be constructed from cold-formed channels, hot rolled sections or built-up welded plate sections, as required.

Purlins and Girds are pre-punched cold-formed “Z & C” shaped sections, 150mm-300mm in depth (based on span) with stiffened flanges.

Fabrication

Our Production team specializes across structural, architectural and precision fabrications each focusing on safe working practice, continuous improvement and quality manufacturing.

Our welders are qualified to the standards required in the industry. Our principle welding processes include submerged Arc, MAG, MIG and CO2.
